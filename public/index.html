> Info

D.A.N. adds more than 30 fun commands to your server for your users to enjoy! When it comes to this bot, the only commands you'll be getting are useless fun ones. Whether you want to throw an insult at a mate, or ask the Magic Conch a question, this bot is sure to fit your needs.

> General
  • -ping - Ping the bot.
  • -help - Display a list of available commands.
  • -who - Get info on the bot.
  • -conch - Ask the magic conch a question.
  • -insult - Insult someone on the server like a 6 year old.
  • -spooky - Check how spooky someone is.
  • -slap - Slap someone on the server.
  • -duel - Duel someone in the server.
  • -ascii <text> - Convert text into ASCII lettering.
  • -horses - Check out the horse races! [COMMAND IS DOWN FOR TIME BEING]
  • -zodiac <month INT> <day INT> - Get info about your zodiac sign.
  • -ow <battle tag> - Get info about your Overwatch profile.
  • -ftoc <number> - Convert Farenheit to Celcius.
  • -ctof <number> - Convert Celcius to Farenheit.
  • -add - Add the bot to your server.
  • -join - Join D.A.N.'s support server.
> Social Stream
  • -social open - Start a Social Stream. (Usable by Role with Manage Messages Permission ONLY)
  • -social close - Close a Social Stream. (Usable by Role with Manage Messages Permission ONLY)
  • -social send - Send a message to other servers through the Social Stream.
  • -streamlist - Check how many servers have a Social Stream open.
> Economy
  • -top <number> - Check the leaderboards and see who's the richest!
  • -payday - Collect your daily paycheck,
  • -balance - Check your bank account balance.
  • -work <job> - Work a job for some extra Coins. Use -jobs to check all available jobs.
  • -jobs - Check all available jobs.
  • -slots - Test your luck, and pull the lever.
  • -heist - Attempt a bank heist.
  • -house - Check on your house.
  • -realestate - Check what houses are up for sale!
  • -buy <number> - Purchase a house. Use -realestate to check available houses.
